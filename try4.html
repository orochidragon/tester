Chapter 1996 Conscription
The ants relied on their numbers to surround the Lickers!

They waved their huge jaws and tore at the Licker’s body.

The Lickers did not have enough power to deal with the advanced game world creatures. Now that they had encountered a novice game, they immediately revealed their ferocious fangs!

The Licker’s claws had additional effects such as sharpness, armor break, and virus. It also inherited some of Fang Heng’s main body’s attributes.

Every time it swung its claws, it could cut a huge wound on the hard ant’s body!

An injury for an injury.

It was not a loss at all!

Gu Qingzhu looked at the two sides that were engaged in battle, and a hint of surprise gradually appeared in her eyes.

The number of Lickers that emerged from the portal was less than a hundred.

However, it could form a line and block the advance of a large group of ants!

Revival ability!

Gu Qingzhu immediately noticed something.

The Licker’s terrifying resurrection ability could completely overcome the attacks of the ant colony. Even if it was surrounded by a large number of hard ants, the Licker was not fatally threatened. It could even maintain 100% of its combat ability!

The battle was extremely fierce. Broken limbs were everywhere, and the air was filled with the thick smell of blood and decay.

The hard ants began to fall one by one.

Gu Qingzhu’s eyelids twitched slightly.

Hmm?

What was that?

She noticed that the corpse of the hard ant was rapidly rotting after its death, and then, large and small calluses quickly condensed from the corpse.

The flesh cocoon seemed to be pregnant with something as if it had a life force that kept contracting, expanding, and jumping.

“Chi, chi chi!”

As a small hole appeared in the meat cocoon, Gu Qingzhu was surprised to find that a zombie creature had crawled out of the cocoon!

The zombie creature’s movements were a little slow. It took some time for it to stand up again. Then, it immediately pounced on the surrounding ant colony!

Gu Qingzhu was surprised by Fang Heng’s ability.

The ability of mutated infection?

What surprised her even more was…

The zombie creatures born from the cocoon perfectly inherited the self-recovery ability of the Licker!

In just a few minutes, as more and more hard ants fell, a large number of zombies were unknowingly born on the battlefield!

In the blink of an eye, the zombie crowd had become the main force against the ant colony!

More than 20 giant hard ants crawled out of the underground crack and immediately charged towards the front of the battlefield!

“Bang! Bang Bang!!!”

The zombie clones blocking the front were knocked away by the giant hard ants due to the impact.

Gu Qingzhu narrowed her eyes. She was about to attack when she saw this, but then she seemed to have noticed something. Her hand stopped in mid-air again.

“Pfft! Tsk tsk!”

The Licker on the battlefield fearlessly leaped onto the giant hard ant!

The Licker’s sharp claws pierced into the hard ant’s body like thorns!
They hung onto the hard ants and attacked them continuously.

In just a short moment, the giant hard ant was crawling with Licker, and its speed gradually slowed down until it completely lost its ability to attack.

Immediately after, a large number of zombie clones swarmed up and completely blocked the giant hard ant’s path of movement!

Other than its huge size, the giant hard ant’s attack ability had been greatly improved. It also had an additional limb severing effect and poison effect.

Every time they brandished their barbed forelimbs, they could cause the enemy to lose limbs.

“What a pity…So it’s not a commander-level creature…”

Fang Heng was a little disappointed and looked away from the giant hard ant.

It was no threat at all!

The two special effects of the elite giant hard ant, limb severing and poison, were countered by the zombie clone’s ‘undying body’ effect.

An ordinary zombie clone could deal with the hard ants without his help.

Just continue to hack according to the original plan.

As the duration of the ultra-small spatial teleportation hub behind them ended, the spatial fluctuations it emitted gradually stopped. It was likely that it would not attract more giant hard ants to take the initiative to attack.

He had to prepare first.

Fang Heng no longer paid attention to the main battlefield and began to adjust his tactics. He sent out a group of Lickers to scout the surroundings, searching for more ant nests and the spatial teleportation passages left behind by the Federation in the game world.

The remaining mutated infected zombie clones stayed behind to continue dealing with the remaining ants on the battlefield.

“Victor.”

“Hu, this world…”

Victor had just entered the world with the last batch of vampires through the ultra-small spatial teleportation hub.

He was wearing a gas mask and a Level 1 protective suit.

Before coming here, Victor heard Fang Heng say that the air in this world was particularly turbid. He was worried that there might be toxins in the air here, so he came here fully armed for safety reasons.

Apart from Victor, more than ten elite mechanical engineers under him also came to help.

It was worth mentioning that ever since Fang Heng took care of the world portal and tried to recruit talents from the Zombie Apocalypse and the Vampire Apocalypse to join the prison, Victor’s newly recruited subordinates could easily be on par with an S-class mechanical NPC.

Of course, thet were still a little inferior to Victor.
